Summary

My name is Apostolos. I am an electrical engineer, a mathematician, musician, and artist.

Background

My engineering background is very broad. I have worked on projects ranging from designing battery management ciruitry onboard mobile robots and laying out circuit boards, to customizing high-voltage vacuum tube audio amplifiers.
For more information, you can check out my resume.

Education

I recieved my masters in electrical engineering from the University of Minnesota in June 2012. My thesis was on modeling energy expenditure in small embedded systems (specifically, mobile robots) and designing a hardware system to facilitate tabulating power dissipation and making sound predictions as to how long such battery-powered systems have until they die. Here is a link to the actual .pdf document: "Energy-Efficient Designs and Principles for Miniature Mobile Robotic Systems."
